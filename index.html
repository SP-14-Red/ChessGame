Our 2024 Senior Project was creating a Chess game with AI

Our senior project is a digital chess game engine with artificial intelligence to provide a fun, yet challenging chess-playing experience. Our program uses complex algorithms such as minimax and alpha-beta pruning to simulate human-like playing capabilities in the AI. This program was made possible using Python and the Pygame library, which offers a great UI, and makes sure to follow all the standard rules of chess, including special moves. This project stands as a great stepping stone when it comes to integrating artificial intelligence into otherwise traditional gaming.
